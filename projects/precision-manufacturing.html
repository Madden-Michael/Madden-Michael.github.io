Precision Manufacturing & Integration

Hands-on experience in precision machining (CNC/manual) and assembly integration. Includes work on thermal systems and maintenance.

Overview

Beyond design and analysis, I have extensive hands-on experience in the machine shop and with hardware integration. Understanding the realities of manufacturing — GD&T, tooling constraints, and assembly stack-ups — makes me a better designer.

Hands-On Machining

I am proficient with both manual and CNC equipment. My experience covers:

  • Mill & Lathe Operations: Custom part fabrication, facing, turning, and precise hole patterns.
  • CNC Programming: CAM toolpath generation and machine setup.
  • Metrology: Verification using calipers, micrometers, and height gauges to ensure parts meet tight tolerances.

Setup and inspection of a precision component.

Thermal System Maintenance

In addition to fabrication, I handle system maintenance and integration tasks. This includes thermal subsystem checks, MLI (Multi-Layer Insulation) handling, and heater circuit validation.

Work on thermal subsystems/maintenance.

Philosophy

"Design for Manufacturing" (DFM) isn't just a checklist item for me; it's a practice rooted in hours spent standing in front of the machine. I design parts that can be made efficiently and inspected reliably.
