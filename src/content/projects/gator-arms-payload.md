### Overview
As a co‑founder and contributor on Orbital Gators, I helped design the GATOR Arms payload — a student‑built payload concept emphasizing mechanical robustness, integration readiness, and a clear test path.

This design was presented with a poster and paper at **SmallSat 2025**.

### Key Contributions
- **Concept Development**: Mechanism concepts, packaging, and integration approach.
- **Design for Build**: Materials selection, tolerance analysis, assembly sequence, and vendor coordination.
- **Test Planning**: Fixture design, inspection criteria, and full acceptance test planning.
